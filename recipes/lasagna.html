Lasagna a la française

Homepage

Description

This recipe adds a nice twist to classic lasagna by using a mix of 7 cheeses.

Ingredients

  • flour
  • water
  • cheese
  • passata
  • olive oil

Steps

  1. Mix flour and water
  2. Dropwise additon of olive oil
  3. Cover in passata and cheese
  4. Bake at 400°C until burnt
